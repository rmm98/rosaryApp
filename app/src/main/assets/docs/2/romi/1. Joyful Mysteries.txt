<b>Sontosache Mister</b> (Somara ani Son’vara)

Bhagevont Khursachye + kurven | nivar amkam + amchya Deva | amchya + dusmanantlim. 
Bapachye | ani Putachye | ani Povitrotmyachye nanvim | Amen.

<b>Sotmantam Devak</b>, | Sorv podvedara Bapak, | Sorga ani pritumechya rochnnarak; | ani Jezu Kristak, | tachya eklyach Putak, amchya Somyak;| To Povitrotmya vorvim gorbim sombovlo,| Ankvar Moriye vorvim zolmolo | Pons Pilata khal tannem koxtt sosle, | taka khursar zoddlo,| to melo, ani taka nikepilo | to melolyam modem denvlo, | tisrya disa melolyantlo jivont zalo | sorgar choddlo, sorv podvedar Deva Bapachya ujvyak bosla | thoim thavn jivyam ani melolyanchi mun’subi korunk yetolo.
Sotmantam Povitrotmyak | katolik povitr sobhek, | bhoktancho ektar,| patkanchem bhogsonnem, kuddichem jivontponn, | sasnnachem jivit. | Amen

<b>Amchya Bapa, Sorginchya | tujem nanv | povitr zaum | tujem raz amkam yenv | tuji khuxi sorgar zata | toxi sonvsarant zanv.</b>
Amcho dispoddto gras az amkam di | ani ami amcher chuklolyank bhogxitanv | toxem amchim patkam bhogos | ani amkam tallnnent poddunk dinvnaka | punn vayttantlim amkam nivar. | Amen.

<b>Noman Moriye, kurpen bhorlolye | Somi tujye sangata | striyam bhitor tum sodenv | ani sodenvi foll tujye kuxichem, Jezu.</b>
Bhagevonti Moriye, Devachye Maye | amam papiam khatir vinoti kor | atam ani amchya mornnachye kallim | Amen.
<b>Mohima Bopak | ani Putak | ani Povitrotmyak; </b>
zoxi adim | toxich atam | ani sodam sorvodam| Amen.

<b>Noman Ranniye</b>
Noman Ranniye, kakulltichye maye; | noman amchya jiva, amruta ani bhorvoxa, tuka ami ulo martanv, ami pordexi Evechim ballkam. | Hya dukhachya konddant astam | askar huskar soddtanv. | tor tum amchye xekaye, | te tujye kakulltiche dolle amchye voir porti | ani hya pordexa uprant | tujye kuxichem sodenvi foll, Jezuk amkam dakoy. | E doyalli, E mogalli, E dulobi Ankvari Moriye.
