<b>Prarthumya</b>

Deva, | tujya eklyach putan | aplye jinniye mornna ani jivantponna vorvim | sasnnachye bachavechem inam | amkam zoddun dovorlam; | Bhagevont Ankvar Moriyeche | bhov povitr Rozariche mister | ami niyalltana, | tantum attaplolyacho ami pattlav koricho | ani tantum bhasaylolem ami zoddixem kor mhonn | ami tujye lagim porat’tanv, | tyach krista amchya somya vorvim. | Amen.
